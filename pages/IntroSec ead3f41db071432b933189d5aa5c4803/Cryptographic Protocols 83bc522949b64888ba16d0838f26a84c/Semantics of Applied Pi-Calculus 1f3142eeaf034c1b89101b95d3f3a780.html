Free- vs. Bound-Names

Free names are public, bound names are (initially) private.

If PPP﻿ is closed, it does not have free variables.

We denote free / bounded names in PPP﻿ as:

fn(P)\text{fn}(P)fn(P)﻿ free name

bn(P)\text{bn}(P)bn(P)﻿ bound name

fv(P)\text{fv}(P)fv(P)﻿ free variable

bv(P)\text{bv}(P)bv(P)﻿ bound variable
